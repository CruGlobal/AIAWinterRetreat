Coach Wooden "Keys to Life" Award

The Coach Wooden "Keys to Life" Award is named in honor of legendary basketball coach John Wooden. During his years at UCLA, Coach wooden guided the Bruins to 10 National Championships (seven consecutive) as well as 19 conference titles.

This Award is presented annually to a member of the college or professional basketball community who lives out qualities exemplefied by Coach Wooden; oustanding character, integrity, and leadership on the court, in the work place, in the home, and in the community.
